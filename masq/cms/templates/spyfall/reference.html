Basic Rules
  • There's a spy in your group, they do NOT know the location you are in.
  • Ask questions of fellow players to determine who the spy is
  • Try to provide the other non-spies hints that you know the location, without giving away the location
  • Remember each non-spy has a different role, so your question may not make sense to someone on your team
    • Game Flow Rules
  • Randomly determine the first player. They choose another player and asks a question.
  • No followup questions are immediately allowed.
  • The player who is answered the question, gets to ask the next question.
  • They can ask any player, except for the last person to ask a question.
  • Play continues until the timer runs out, everyone accuses one person of being a spy, or the spy figures out the location.
    • End of Round
  • Rounds can end in a multiple of ways:
  • 8 minute timer elapses (or whatever time that's chosen by the group)
  • The spy stops the game to guess the location (positive or negative)
  • A player accuse a single player of being a spy and all other players agree
  • Start Timer - Whoever has access starts the timer in the app. (Skip if it's the last round)
  • Players return to their room as the next round as started
    • Scoring Rules
  • The game ends after the hostage exchange of the last round
  • Some roles have an End Game Priority Numbers.
  • Starting with the Lowest number have each of those characters handle their end game action in turn
  • After each of those have been resolved, each player reveals their role in their room.
  • At this point the Bomber detonates his bomb, giving everyone the dead condition in that room
  • Now check each player's role, and determine who has won!
